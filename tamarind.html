TAMARIND DESIGNS

Background

An Ann Arbor Bulk Goods store needed a brand overhaul to entice customers

Solution

An identity package including logo, typeface selection, colors, and mobile view was created

Logo

The Logo for this identity represents a bag of bulk goods. It is comprised of symmetric shapes.

Typography

The Typeface selected for this identity is Arboria Book. It is simple yet elegant which matches the brand image. The secondary typeface is the serif Dejanire, which pairs well with Arboria due to its matching minimalism.

Design & Colors

The Primary Colors selected for this identity include muted versions of: gold to represent quality, green to repsent ecofriendly, and brown to represent sustainability. A secondary palatte of greens, blues, and purples was also picked to pair with the primary palatte.

Free Space

The freespace selected for the logomark is 1/3 the logomark width. For all other versions, it is one content ball.
